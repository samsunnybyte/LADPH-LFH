Here’s What Others Have to Say

Not sure what to expect? Still have questions about how the program works? Hear straight from LA County residents who have participated in the program.




Safe Home During Pregnancy

Jamie is pregnant with her third child and was aware of lead poisoning but learned so much more about how damaging lead paint hazards can be to her family after participating in the program. She also appreciated being able to provide input during the remediation process.



Restoring a home that is more than 100 years old

Jackie’s home is more than 100 years old. She is impressed with the level of precautions the team took to ensure her home would be safely remediated from lead paint hazards.



The program addressed Nathan’s concerns and informed him that the program is completely free.

Nathan is a father to three young children. His wife was pregnant at the time of remediation, and they had some initial reservations about the program. After connecting with the assigned project manager and crew, they felt reassured about the program.



Communication with the contractors was clear and on schedule

Tim and Liz were relieved to know that their home is free of lead-paint hazards, making it a safer environment for their young children. They were positively surprised with how easy the remediation process was.



The Lead Free Homes LA program has created a safer home for Paul and his family.

Paul has two young children living in his home and was glad that all lead-paint hazards have been removed from the outside and inside of his home, providing a safer home environment for his children.



The Lead Free Homes LA crew was very accommodating and professional.

Ted was very content with the professionalism of the crew. He says the team went above and beyond to repair his home and is very happy with what was accomplished.



Outreach Program Success Story

Oscar talks about the trust and rapport our program representatives built with him to ensure that all his questions and concerns were answered throughout each step of the process.



Providing a Safe Environment

Eloisa was satisfied with the repairs performed in her home and talked about the safety precautions our program staff took during remediation. She is happy that the program has made her house a safe and lead-free environment for her two grandsons.



Recommending the Program

It was important for Monica to enroll in this program because she was concerned for the health of her daughter and family members. She is happy with the repairs and has recommended the Lead Free Homes LA program to her friends and neighbors.



Our program provides financial assistance for relocation

Gerardo is glad our program provides lead-paint remediation services for free and financial support for temporary relocation needs if needed.



Quick and Polished Repairs

Herminia is very happy with how quickly the repairs were done and how well the work turned out. She has peace of mind knowing that her grandchildren who she sees frequently are safe to visit her home.



Grateful and Reassured for her Health

Maria is grateful for California State funded programs like Lead Free Homes LA that have helped to restore the health and safety of her home. She has also recommended the program to her friends and family.



Making It Safer to Stay Safe at Home

Alejandro’s home was remediated during the COVID-19 pandemic. His repair team took every precaution to keep Alejandro safe while removing lead-based paint hazards.



Savings and Safety in Boyle Heights

Merle and her son appreciated the flexible scheduling of the repairs and the financial assistance that covered all repair and relocation costs during a stressful time.

*This person participated in a HUD-funded program under the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health, which is similar to the Lead Free Homes LA Program.



No Days Off

Howard was impressed by the hard work and dedication of his repair team to finish the work quickly with attention to quality.



Gratitude for the Future

Eulalio’s granddaughters can now come to visit him at home with the certainty that there is no longer any harmful lead.



Safe and Beautiful

Nicador was pleased with the results: a home that is free of lead paint hazards and looks great with fresh paint and new windows.
